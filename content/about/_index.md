## About this project

A practice repo for the **Claude Certified Architect — Foundations (CCA-F)** exam, built around a `CLAUDE.md` that turns Claude itself into a section-by-section mock-exam proctor. The same study sections that power the mock exam are published here as a browsable reference.

The repo and site are open source under the **MIT License**. Pull requests, corrections, and additional questions are welcome.

- **Source:** [github.com/ai-1st/claude-certified-architect](https://github.com/ai-1st/claude-certified-architect)
- **License:** MIT — see [`LICENSE`](https://github.com/ai-1st/claude-certified-architect/blob/main/LICENSE)
- **Status:** Unofficial. Not affiliated with or endorsed by Anthropic.
